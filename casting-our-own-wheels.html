Casting Our Own Wheels

How to Cast Your Own Robot Wheels

Learn how to design, mold, and cast custom wheels for your robotics projects. This guide will walk you through the process, materials, and tips for success.

  • Design: Sketch or CAD your wheel shape and size.
  • Mold Making: Use 3D printing, laser cutting, or manual methods to create a mold.
  • Materials: Choose silicone, urethane, or rubber compounds for casting.
  • Casting: Mix and pour your material into the mold, let it cure.
  • Finishing: Trim, drill, and fit your wheels to your robot.

Tips: Use release agents for easy demolding, experiment with different compounds for grip, and always wear safety gear!
